Research PROJECTS

These are the projects I've been involved in throughout my research journey.

BIOCOMPOSITE DEVELOPMEMT

Developed eco-friendly composite materials by incorporating algae cellulose to enhance the mechanical and thermal properties of thermoplastic polymers for sustainable packaging applications.

Learn more

NANOCELLULOSE PRODUCTION

Investigated the production and characterization of algae-derived TEMPO-oxidized nanocellulose, demonstrating high crystallinity and thermal stability for use as reinforcement fillers.

Learn more

Algae Cellulose Extraction and Characterization

Extracted and characterized cellulose from New Zealand native algae species, assessing their high crystallinity, thermal stability, and potential for use in sustainable composite materials.

Learn more

Cellulose and Biostimulant from Oedogonium grown in wastewater

Developed a two-step biorefinery process using Oedogonium calcareum biomass from wastewater treatment to extract biostimulants and high-purity cellulose.

Learn more

Novel 2,6-pyridinedicarboxamide-1,2,3-triazole Ligands for Luminescent Lanthanide Materials

Synthesized novel 2,6-pyridyldicarboxamide-derived ligands using CuAAC reaction to form luminescent complexes with europium and terbium for potential biological and sensing applications.
